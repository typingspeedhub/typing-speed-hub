Typing Statistics

Typing statistics help you understand how fast people type, what counts as a good typing speed, and how performance varies across different professions and age groups. By analyzing typing data, you can set realistic goals and track your improvement over time.

Most people type between 30 and 50 words per minute (WPM), while professionals often reach 60–80 WPM or more. However, speed alone is not everything — accuracy and consistency play a key role in efficient typing. Why Typing Speed Matters

Typing speed is an important skill in today’s digital world. Faster typing can improve productivity, reduce time spent on repetitive tasks, and help you communicate more efficiently. Whether you are a student, developer, writer, or office worker, improving your typing speed can have a measurable impact on your daily work.
