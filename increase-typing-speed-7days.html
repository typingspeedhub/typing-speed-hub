How to Increase Typing Speed in 7 Days

📅 Day 1: Establish Your Baseline

Take a typing test to determine your current Words Per Minute (WPM) and accuracy. Note your result to track progress.

📚 Day 2: Learn Proper Finger Placement

Use a finger chart and practice home-row positioning. Avoid peeking at the keyboard while typing.

⌨️ Day 3: Practice with Short Sentences

Type simple sentences focusing on accuracy over speed. Do this for at least 10 minutes.

⏱ Day 4: Timed Typing Tests

Take 1-minute typing tests and focus on improving WPM. Don’t rush—accuracy comes first.

🧠 Day 5: Learn to Type Common Words

Practice frequently used words and word combinations to boost familiarity and speed.

🎯 Day 6: Reduce Errors

Review your past test results and identify repeated mistakes. Focus today’s practice on avoiding those errors.

🏁 Day 7: Final Test and Reflection

Take a final test and compare it with Day 1. Celebrate your improvement and commit to continued practice.

✅ Summary

Increasing typing speed in a week is possible with consistency, focus on accuracy, and mindful daily practice. Keep going beyond day 7!
