What You Can Contact Us About

  • Suggestions for new typing tools, calculators, or educational topics
  • Questions about privacy, cookies, analytics, or data handling
  • Business partnerships, advertising opportunities, affiliate partnerships, or sponsorship inquiries

Reporting a Technical Problem

When reporting a problem, please include the name or URL of the affected page and a short description of what happened. Information about your browser, device, or the steps that caused the issue may also help us understand and correct the problem.

Corrections and Content Feedback

We aim to keep our typing resources clear, accurate, and useful. If you notice outdated information, a calculation problem, a broken link, or an explanation that could be improved, please let us know.

Pages may be reviewed and updated when corrections are needed or when additional information would make the content more useful to visitors.

About Typing Speed Hub

Typing Speed Hub is an independent educational website focused on typing tests, WPM calculations, typing accuracy, practice drills, performance analysis, statistics, and practical guides.

The website is designed for students, beginners, office workers, remote workers, and anyone who wants to improve typing speed and accuracy through simple and accessible resources.

We are not affiliated with any keyboard manufacturer, typing software company, school, certification provider, or testing organization.

We usually respond within 1–2 business days, although response times may occasionally be longer during busy periods.

We do not use a contact form because direct email communication is simpler and helps us avoid collecting unnecessary information through the website.
