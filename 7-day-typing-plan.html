7-Day Typing Practice Plan

Ready to improve your typing skills in just one week? This step-by-step plan is designed to help you build accuracy, speed, and confidence through daily practice.

Day 1 – Mastering the Home Row

Focus on finger placement. Use beginner drills to practice the ASDF JKL; keys without looking. Build muscle memory from the ground up.

Tip: Sit straight, relax your wrists, and keep your fingers lightly on the keys.

Day 2 – Introducing the Top and Bottom Rows

Add the top row (QWERTYUIOP) and bottom row (ZXCVBNM) keys. Practice transitions between rows while maintaining accuracy.

Tip: Don’t rush. Focus on steady rhythm over speed.

Day 3 – Typing Short Sentences

Practice short, real-world sentences using all letter keys. Accuracy is still more important than speed.

Motivation: Small wins today build momentum for tomorrow.

Day 4 – Increasing Speed with Repetition

Use timed exercises (1-3 minutes) to build speed. Repeat challenging words until they become effortless.

Tip: Use typing tools or games that measure your Words Per Minute (WPM).

Day 5 – Real-World Practice

Type emails, notes, or articles. Avoid copy-pasting—type everything manually. This simulates actual use and improves fluency.

Motivation: You’re more than halfway there—keep pushing!

Day 6 – Typing Under Pressure

Try typing with a timer or while multitasking. Practice focus and accuracy under light stress, like background noise or music.

Tip: Stay calm. Mistakes are part of the learning process.

Day 7 – Full Practice and Reflection

Take a 5-minute typing test. Compare your WPM and accuracy with Day 1. Reflect on your progress and celebrate your effort!

Motivation: You’ve built a new habit in just 7 days. Keep going!
