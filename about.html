About Typing Speed Hub

Typing Speed Hub is a free typing practice website built to help you improve your typing speed (WPM) and accuracy with simple tools and practical guides.

What we offer

  • Typing tests with WPM and accuracy tracking
  • Short daily drills (beginner to advanced)
  • Helpful articles and tips to build real typing habits
  • Kid-friendly practice ideas and learning resources

Whether you’re a student, a remote worker, or a complete beginner, our goal is to keep typing practice simple, effective, and accessible — no account required.

This site is managed by an independent creator focused on clarity, privacy, and usability. We do not sell personal data, and we aim to keep the experience clean and user-friendly.
