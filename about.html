About Typing Speed Hub

Typing Speed Hub was founded with a single goal: to make typing practice simple, effective, and accessible to everyone — no account needed, no distractions, just results.

Whether you're a student preparing for exams, a remote worker trying to boost productivity, or a complete beginner, our tools and guides are designed to help you type faster and more accurately with just a few minutes of daily effort.

This site is managed by a small, independent creator with a background in digital learning tools. Every article and typing aid is written and built with clarity, privacy, and usability in mind.

We don’t use intrusive ads or sell your data. Our mission is to support learners by offering free, trustworthy resources.

Have feedback or questions? Feel free to reach out at typingspeedhub@gmail.com.
