Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Typing Speed Hub (https://typingspeedhub.com) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how cookies and advertising technologies may be used, and what choices you have when using our website.

For the purpose of applicable data protection laws, Typing Speed Hub is the data controller of personal data that we may process through this website.

2. Information We Collect Automatically

When you visit Typing Speed Hub, certain technical information may be collected automatically. This may include:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Referring pages or search terms, where available
  • General location information such as country or region

This information is used for analytics, security, performance monitoring, fraud prevention, and improving the website experience.

3. Personal Information You Provide

Typing Speed Hub does not require user registration. We do not ask users to create accounts in order to use our typing test or read our content.

We may collect personal information only if you voluntarily provide it, for example when you contact us by email. This may include your name, email address, and the content of your message.

4. Typing Test Data

Typing Speed Hub may process typing test results such as words per minute (WPM), accuracy, errors, test duration, and performance-related statistics. This information is used to provide the typing test functionality and improve the quality of our tools.

Unless clearly stated otherwise, typing test results are not linked to a registered user account, because registration is not required.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Typing Speed Hub may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to operate the website, remember preferences, analyze traffic, improve functionality, prevent abuse, and support advertising.

Cookies are small files stored on your device. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that some parts of the website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

6. Analytics

We may use analytics services, such as Google Analytics or similar tools, to understand how visitors use our website. Analytics services may collect information such as pages visited, session duration, device type, browser type, and approximate location.

This information helps us improve content, tools, performance, and user experience.

7. Advertising Partners

Typing Speed Hub may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners. These partners may include Google AdSense, Media.net, or other advertising networks if they are approved and implemented on the website.

Third-party advertising partners may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, device identifiers, or similar technologies to serve ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, limit how often you see an ad, and provide relevant or contextual advertising.

Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings. You can also learn more about how Google uses information from sites and apps that use its services by visiting Google's partner sites information page.

8. Affiliate Links

Some pages on Typing Speed Hub may contain affiliate links. If you click an affiliate link and purchase a product or service, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

Affiliate links help support the operation of the website. We aim to recommend only relevant products or services related to typing, keyboards, productivity, education, or learning.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we process personal data only where we have a legal basis to do so. These legal bases may include:

  • Your consent, for example for certain cookies or personalized advertising where required

You may be able to accept, reject, or manage certain categories of cookies and advertising preferences through the cookie consent tool shown on the website, where available.
