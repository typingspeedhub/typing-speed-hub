Common Typing Mistakes and How to Fix Them

Whether you're a beginner or someone trying to improve your typing speed, certain mistakes tend to appear over and over again. Recognizing these issues early on can help you become a faster, more accurate typist.

1. Looking at the Keyboard

❌ Why it’s a problem: This slows you down and makes it harder to develop true muscle memory.

✅ Fix it: Practice touch typing with home-row based lessons (ASDF – JKL;) and cover your hands if necessary. Use typing tools that provide on-screen keyboard guidance.
