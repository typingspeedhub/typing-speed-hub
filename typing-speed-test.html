7-Day Plan to Increase Typing Speed and Accuracy

Looking to type faster and more accurately without spending hours every day? This focused 7-day routine is designed to give you a realistic boost in WPM while building habits that keep paying off long after the week is over.

How This 7-Day Typing Speed Plan Works

This plan helps you:

  • Build consistent daily typing habits
  • Train proper finger placement and posture
  • Increase WPM without sacrificing accuracy
  • Identify and fix your most common mistakes
  • Create a simple routine you can repeat weekly
